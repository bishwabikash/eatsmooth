FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQs)

I am facing an issue with website/app.
Please write to us at service@eatsmooth.com with all the details.

Can I place an order over a phone?
Yes, you can. Call us at +917002783224

Which are the cities you currently serve?
Currently we are serving in Silchar only.

Will you be responsible for the quality of the food?
We only collect food on your behalf from the outlets and deliver it, and we won’t be accountable for the quality of the food. In case you have any feedback, please write in through the contact us page and we’ll take it up with the restaurant in question. Don't forget to tell us the order number or restaurant name.

How do I contact Eat Smooth customer care?
You can always reach us through chat option on our website, write to us at service@eatsmooth.com or call us at +917002783224

I am a restaurant owner, how do I tie-up with Eat Smooth?
You can always reach us through chat option on our website, write to us at service@eatsmooth.com or call us at +917002783224. Our sales team will get in touch with you post reviewing the same.

What is the minimum order value for food which you deliver?
We do not have any minimum order value for delivery.

Can I order across neighbourhoods/any area?
We will deliver from any restaurant listed on our service, as long as the PIN code is valid in our website/app for your location.

What are your delivery hours?
Our delivery hours are subjected to the timings of each restaurant.

Do you charge for the delivery?
We charge a delivery fee for orders below a certain amount in certain areas. The exact amount will reflect in your cart at the checkout page.

How much time do you take to deliver?
This is usually between 30 to 60 minutes. We mention estimated time on details of every restaurant

Can I order from multiple restaurant in a single order?
No. However, you can place orders for individual items from different restaurants as we do not impose a minimum order value.

Can I order in advance?
No, you cannot order in advance. All our orders are placed and executed on-demand.

How can I add special instructions to my order?
Once you have chosen the restaurant you want to order from, add items to your cart and proceed. After that you can call us and instruct us for which you may get charged extra. However we are unable to guarantee these requests to be fulfilled as it solely depends on the restaurants.

How can I track my order?
We will keep you posted through SMS with delivery boy details. The progress of your order will be shared with you through SMS, along every step between order placements to delivery.

My order is delayed.
If you have received an SMS with the contact details of our delivery person you can call our delivery executive and ask him about his location and order status. If you are not satisfied, you can chat/call us to know the status.

I want to change my delivery address / mobile number.
Any major change in delivery address is not possible after you have placed an order with us. However, slight modifications are allowed like changing flat number, street name, landmark etc. Request you to get in touch with us. If you have received delivery person details, you can directly call him.

What is Eat Smooth cancellation policy?
We do allow cancelling an order before it is prepared by the restaurant. It is not possible for us to cancel an order once it is prepared by the restaurant. Usually this is within 5 minutes of placing an order.

I did not receive the complete order.
If certain items are missing from your order, please check if the item has been billed or not. Please call us at +917002783224 or email us at service@eatsmooth.com with your order id and other details.

Do I need to register for placing an order?
Yes.

I am unable to login to my account.
Try the 'Forgot password' option. If you are still facing trouble logging in then write to us at service@eatsmooth.com with your registered email or call us with your registered mobile number at +917002783224.

Are your menu prices the same as those at the restaurant?
We encourage our restaurants to always use the same pricing for delivery as they do for their in-house menu, although there may be exceptions. If you have any questions about menu pricing, please contact the restaurant directly.

What are the payment options available for me?
You are currently operating only through Cash on Delivery.

What is the packaging charge which is mentioned on the bill?
The packaging charge is collected by the restaurant on each order placed for takeaway or delivery.
